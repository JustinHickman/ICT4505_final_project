Welcome to SigEp

Welcome to the homepage of the Texas Kappa Chapter of the Sigma Phi Epsilon Fraternity at The University of Texas at Arlington. Since 1971, our chapter has prided itself on building balanced men and forging lifelong friendships, while upholding the ideals of Sigma Phi Epsilon.

We currently have over 40 active brothers on campus and are always looking to recruit a new class of ambitious young men. The Chapter is also supported by a thriving network of alumni who serve as mentors for numerous brothers.
